Mission

Deliver fast, reliable fraud prevention for UPI transactions with clear and actionable risk awareness.

Vision

Build a trusted digital payment ecosystem where transactions are safer and users transact with confidence.

Services

  • Transaction amount risk-level guidance
  • Transfer-limit awareness and rule alerts
  • Simple decision support for safer UPI usage

Project Brief

This project secures UPI payments with registration-based access, risk evaluation, and additional verification for suspicious transfers.

Risk Levels

  • Low (0 - 10,000): Transaction allowed directly.
  • Medium (10,001 - 50,000): OTP verification required via registered email.
  • High (50,001 - 100,000): OTP via email plus security notification alert.
  • Blocked (Above 100,000): Transfer is denied.
